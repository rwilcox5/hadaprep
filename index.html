How does Hadaprep work?

Choose a test you are studying for. Choose a question. Answer the question to the best of your ability. Get a score that compares your answer to everone else's answer. Repeat. Track your progress in several areas to focus on your weaknesses.

Learn more

What is the catch?

In order to get feedback for your answer you must grade the answers from other students. You will be given two answers and asked to choose which answer is better. Don't worry about assigning a numerical grade or providing in-depth analysis. Just pick which answer is better. Repeat a few times to earn enough points to get your answers graded. While reading what other students wrote you should be able to improve yourself.
